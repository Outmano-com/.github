## What is Outmano?

Outmano watches every competitor's **pricing, SEO, content, roadmap, and reviews**, then tells you what moved, why it matters, and what to do next — so you're never blindsided, and you're always the one moving first.

Dedicated AI agents monitor each angle, filter out the noise, and deliver a plain-English strategic read to your inbox every Monday and to your team's Slack the moment something shifts. The competitive read a CI analyst would give you — without hiring one. **Five minutes a week.**

## Intelligence angles

Each "angle" is a focused lens with its own data-collection tooling and a specialized AI agent. A meta-agent then synthesizes across angles into each competitor's overall trajectory — the *"so what"* layer.

| Angle | What it watches |
| --- | --- |
| **Pricing** | Tier and plan changes, repositioning, and where you're over- or under-priced against your own plans. |
| **SEO** | Organic keywords, top pages, traffic value, and share of search — with your overlap front and center. |
| **Content** | Blog posts and announcements, summarized — surfacing only what signals a real strategic move. |
| **Roadmap** | Public roadmaps and changelogs, flagging the moment a feature moves from *planned* to *shipped*. |
| **Reviews** | Rating trends, new-review alerts, and recurring complaints their customers have that you do better. |

New angles ship every month and are included in your existing plan. *Hiring signals are next.*

## How it works

1. **Add a competitor** — drop in a name and the pages to watch (pricing, blog, roadmap). Add your own product too, and Outmano tracks the gap. ~30 seconds.
2. **AI agents monitor and analyze** — each angle is tracked by a dedicated agent that explains what happened and why it matters when something shifts.
3. **Read your Monday briefing** — one email with all competitor activity, grouped by competitor. Your 5-minute competitive edge.
